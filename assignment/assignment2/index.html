About Brody

Brody is a 2.5 year old Golden Retriever/Irish Setter mix. His first family gave him up becase they couldn't handle the energy of a Golden Retriever puppy. They took him to Delaware Valley Golden Retriever Rescue. He was there for a few months before he was adopted by his forever family when he was a year old. He is a very sweet and happy boy who loves to snuggle.
